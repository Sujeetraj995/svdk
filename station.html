Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra Railway Station

Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra railway station is a railway station on the Jammu Udhampur Srimage.pnginagar Baramulla Railway Link in the Indian Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir. The ground floor of Katra railway station has escalators, lifts, current reservation, second class booking, train enquiry section, pilgrim guide, tourist assistance, VIP lounge, a fully air-conditioned hotel with a shopping lounge, multi-cuisine restaurant, cloak room, waiting hall, a book stall, tea stall, toilet blocks, and catering area. The first floor accommodates eight retiring rooms and a cafeteria.[6] A huge parking place also constructed to accommodate cars and passenger buses. Being a modern railway station.
